Otto Capture uses AI to extract structured data from receipts, bills, and invoices. Once extracted, you review the data and publish it to your accounting platform.

## Setup steps

<Steps>
    1. **Connect your accounting platform:** Authorise Capture to access your client's accounting data. This syncs contacts, chart of accounts, tax rates, and currencies.

    2. **Configure your client:** Set an email prefix for email submissions, choose a default bill state, and review the synced accounting data.

    3. **Submit documents:** Upload files through the web interface or forward emails to your client's unique email address.

    4. **Review and publish:** Check the AI-extracted data, make corrections if needed, and publish to your accounting platform.

</Steps>

## Submission methods

- **Web upload:** Drag and drop files or use the file picker in the Capture interface
- **Email:** Forward receipts and invoices to your client's dedicated email address

## Supported file types

Capture accepts the following file formats:

| Category  | Formats               |
| --------- | --------------------- |
| Documents | PDF                   |
| Images    | JPEG, PNG, TIFF, WebP |
| Web pages | HTML                  |

<Aside type="tip">
  For best results, submit clear, high-resolution images or native PDFs rather
  than scanned documents.
</Aside>

## What happens after submission

See [Submitting documents](/capture/getting-started/submitting-documents) for full details on the processing pipeline and document statuses. In summary, every document goes through a malware scan, AI classification and extraction, validation, and contact matching before appearing in your [review queue](/capture/guides/reviewing).
