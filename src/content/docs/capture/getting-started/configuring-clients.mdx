Each client in Capture represents a single accounting platform organisation. After connecting your accounting platform, you can configure how documents are processed and published for that client.

## Email prefix

Each client has a unique email address for receiving documents by email. The address follows the format:

```
{prefix}@{your-organisation}.ottocapture.email
```

For example, if your organisation is "smith-accounting" and you set the prefix to "acme", the email address would be `acme@smith-accounting.ottocapture.email`.

Email prefixes:

- Must start with a letter or number
- Can contain lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens, dots, and underscores
- Must be unique within your organisation

<Aside>
  If your organisation has a [custom email
  domain](/capture/guides/custom-email-domain) configured, documents can be sent
  to `{prefix}@{your - custom - domain}`. Your `ottocapture.email` address
  continues to work alongside the custom domain.
</Aside>

## Default publish state

On platforms that support settable publish states, you choose the state Otto creates new bills and sales invoices in, and you can override it for a single document during review. Today that's Xero, which has three states: **Draft**, **Awaiting approval**, and **Awaiting payment**. See [Xero publish states](/capture/platforms/xero#publish-states) for what each one means. If you're not sure which to choose, start with **Draft**: you can change it later, and it gives you the most control before anything is sent or paid.

<Aside type="caution" title="Please note">
  FreeAgent has no settable publish state, so this option doesn't appear for
  FreeAgent clients. Bills enter the ledger as soon as they're published, and
  sales invoices are created as Draft. See [publishing to
  FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent#publishing).
</Aside>

## Synced accounting data

Capture syncs the following from your client's accounting platform. This data is used during extraction and review to ensure documents are published with valid accounting data.

  src={accountingPlatformSettings}
  alt="Client accounting platform settings and information"
/>

### Account codes

The list of accounts you code line items to is synced from your accounting platform: Xero calls it the chart of accounts, FreeAgent calls them categories. During extraction, Otto suggests account codes (nominal codes on FreeAgent) based on the document content and how you've coded similar documents for this client before. During review, you can change the code from a list of valid accounts. See [Xero](/capture/platforms/xero#terminology) and [FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent#what-syncs-after-connecting) for the exact terms.

Accounts are filtered by type based on the document:

- **Bills** show expense accounts (Direct Costs and Overheads in Xero)
- **Sales invoices** show revenue accounts

### Tax rates

Tax rates are synced from your accounting platform and applied to line items. Otto suggests tax rates during extraction, and you can adjust them during review. Tax rates are filtered to show only those applicable to the document type.

### Currencies

If your client's accounting platform supports multiple currencies, Capture syncs the available currencies. Otto detects the document currency during extraction.

### Tracking categories

Tracking categories apply to platforms that support them. Xero tracking categories and their options are synced, and you can assign them to line items during review. FreeAgent has no tracking categories. See [Xero tracking categories](/capture/platforms/xero#tracking-categories).

## Refreshing synced data

If you've made changes in your accounting platform (added new accounts, updated tax rates, etc.), you can trigger a manual sync from the client configuration page. You can sync individual data types, or all of them.
