Otto Capture publishes finished bills and invoices to your client's accounting platform. Capture supports Xero and FreeAgent, and each client connects to a single accounting platform organisation. This page covers the parts of connecting that work the same way on every platform. The exact authorisation steps, the terms you'll see, and a few behaviours differ by platform, so for the specifics head to the [Xero](/capture/platforms/xero) or [FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent) page.

## Adding a client

<Steps>
    1. From the **Clients** page in Capture, click **Add client**

    2. Choose the accounting platform you want to connect

    3. Authorise Capture on the platform's sign-in screen

    4. You'll be redirected back to Capture, and data sync begins automatically

</Steps>

## What Capture syncs

Once a client is connected, Capture reads the reference data it needs to process and publish documents:

- **Contacts:** to match suppliers and customers on your documents, and to create new ones during review.
- **Account codes:** the list of accounts or categories you code line items to.
- **Tax rates:** the rates Otto suggests and validates against.
- **Currencies:** the currencies the organisation can use.
- **Organisation details:** base currency, tax registration, and other settings Otto needs to process documents correctly.

The names of some of these differ by platform (Xero's "chart of accounts" is FreeAgent's "categories", for example), and a few are platform-specific. Your platform page lists exactly what syncs.

## Connection status

Each client shows one of four connection statuses on the **Clients** page and on the client settings page:

| Status                | Meaning                                                                                                  |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Active**            | Connected and syncing. Otto can publish bills and invoices.                                              |
| **Pending setup**     | Connected, but setup isn't finished. Finish setup before submitting documents.                           |
| **Disconnected**      | The connection has been removed, either by you in Capture or, on some platforms, on the platform's side. |
| **Connection failed** | Capture tried to sync or publish and the platform rejected the connection. Reconnect to resume.          |

An **Active** client shows its connected organisation name on the client settings page, along with the last sync time.

A **Pending setup** client shows a **Complete setup** action in the **Needs setup** list on the **Clients** page, so you can finish connecting it before submitting documents.

<Aside type="tip">
  If synced data looks outdated, you can trigger a manual refresh from the
  client configuration page.
</Aside>

## Disconnecting and reconnecting

You can disconnect a client at any time from the client settings. Disconnecting:

- Stops all data syncing between Capture and the platform
- Prevents new documents from being published
- Does **not** delete any documents already in Capture
- Does **not** remove any bills or invoices already published to the platform

To reconnect, open the client settings and click **Reconnect**. Clients that need attention also show a **Reconnect** action in the **Needs setup** list on the **Clients** page. Reconnecting triggers a fresh data sync, and Otto resumes publishing. Your existing documents and synced data are kept throughout.

How a connection comes to be revoked, and whether revoking it inside the platform is mirrored back into Capture, depends on the platform. See the [Xero](/capture/platforms/xero) and [FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent) pages for the details.
