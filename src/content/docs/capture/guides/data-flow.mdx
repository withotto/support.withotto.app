This page gives a high-level picture of the data that moves between Otto Capture and your accounting platform, and sets out what Capture does and doesn't change. The exact data read and written, and the names each platform uses, differ by platform, so for the precise lists see the [Xero](/capture/platforms/xero) and [FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent) pages.

## What Capture reads

To process and publish documents, Capture reads reference data from your client's accounting platform: contacts, the accounts or categories you code line items to, tax rates, currencies, and basic organisation details such as base currency and tax registration. Xero clients also sync tracking categories, which FreeAgent doesn't have. This data is used only to suggest and validate values during extraction and review. For the exact fields and the names each platform uses, see what syncs on the [Xero](/capture/platforms/xero#what-syncs-after-connecting) and [FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent#what-syncs-after-connecting) pages.

### Sync timing

- **On connection:** A full sync runs immediately when you connect a client
- **Manual refresh:** You can trigger a sync from the client configuration page at any time

## What Capture publishes

When you publish a document, Capture creates a bill or sales invoice on your platform, carrying the line items, tax, currency, due date, and the matched contact. If you added a new contact during review, that contact is created first. The original document is attached where the platform supports it. Publish states and attachment limits differ by platform: see publishing to [Xero](/capture/platforms/xero#publish-states) and [FreeAgent](/capture/platforms/freeagent#publishing).

## What Capture does NOT modify

Capture has a limited scope within your accounting platform. It does **not**:

- Modify or delete existing bills, invoices, or contacts
- Access payroll or employee data
- Modify your chart of accounts or categories, tax rates, or any settings
- Access reports or financial statements
- Delete any data

Capture only **reads** reference data (contacts, accounts, tax rates) and **creates** new bills, invoices, contacts, and attachments.
