Organisation settings control how your Capture organisation appears, who has access, and how email is configured. Access these from **Settings** in the navigation.

## General

### Organisation name

Your organisation name is displayed throughout the Capture interface.

### Organisation slug

The slug is used in your default email addresses:

```
{prefix}@{slug}.ottocapture.email
```

Changing the slug updates all default email addresses for your clients.

<Aside type="caution">
  If you change your slug, the previous email addresses will stop working. Make
  sure to update any email forwarding rules that use the old addresses.
</Aside>

## Team

You can invite team members to your organisation and manage their access from **Settings → Team**.

### Roles

| Role       | Description                                                                               |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Admin**  | Full access to all settings, clients, and team management                                 |
| **Member** | Can review and publish documents, but cannot manage organisation settings or team members |

### Inviting team members

Invite new team members by entering their email address from the **Settings → Team** page. Invitations expire if not accepted.

### Removing team members

Admins can remove team members from the organisation at any time. Removing a team member revokes their access immediately.

## Email

You can configure a custom email domain for your organisation. This lets clients receive documents at addresses on your own domain instead of the default `ottocapture.email` addresses.

See [Custom email domain](/capture/guides/custom-email-domain) for setup instructions.
